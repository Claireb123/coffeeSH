Where Coffee and Friends Make The Perfect Blend!


At Hit Me Up Coffee, we're passionate about serving exceptional coffee in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Located in the heart of Da Nang, our coffee shop is more than just a place to grab your morning brew; it's a community hub where friends, families, and colleagues come together to relax, recharge, and reconnect.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where everyone feels welcomed, whether you're a coffee aficionado or just starting your coffee journey. With our carefully curated selection of specialty coffees sourced from sustainable and ethical producers, every cup tells a story of quality, craftsmanship, and dedication.

But it's not just about the coffee; it's about the experience. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be greeted with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warm smiles of our friendly baristas. Whether you're looking for your favorite espresso drink or a refreshing cold brew - we've got you covered.

So whether you're stopping by for your daily caffeine fix, catching up with friends, or simply looking for a quiet place to work, we invite you to join us at Hit Me Up Coffee, where every cup is brewed with love and served with a smile.
